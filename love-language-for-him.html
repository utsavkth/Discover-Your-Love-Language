The 5 Love Languages® Profile for Couples—for Him

Complete the quiz to discover how you connect with your partner and feel most loved!

Instructions

As you take this quiz, think specifically about your relationship with your partner:

  • Consider how you feel most loved and appreciated in your marriage or committed relationship.
  • Choose the option that resonates most with how you experience affection and connection from your partner.
  • If both options feel relevant, select the one that has a more significant impact on your relationship.
  • This quiz is meant to help you better understand your love language within a committed partnership.

Enjoy discovering your love language!

Your Privacy Matters

Your quiz results are not shared with anyone unless you choose to do so. The website doesn't save any responses; it is simply to help you understand your love language and relationships.

At the end of the quiz, you will be able to download your results or share them with your loved ones to enhance your relationship.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I choose between the two options for each question? Select the option that resonates most with you in the context of your current relationships.
  • What if both options feel relevant to me? It’s common to relate to multiple love languages. Choose the one that feels more significant or impactful for you right now.
  • Why do some of the options seem to repeat? I intentionally designed the quiz to repeat similar options in different contexts. This helps to ensure more accurate and consistent results, allowing you to discover your primary love language more effectively.
  • How long will the quiz take? The quiz typically takes about 5-10 minutes, but take your time to reflect on each question.
  • Will my responses be kept confidential? Yes, your answers are not saved or shared with anyone unless you choose to share your results. This quiz is for your personal insight.
