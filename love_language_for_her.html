The 5 Love Languages® Profile for Couples—for Her

Complete the quiz to discover how you connect with your partner and feel most loved!

Instructions

As you take this quiz, think specifically about your relationship with your partner:

  • Reflect on how you feel most loved and valued in your marriage or committed relationship.
  • Choose the option that resonates most with how you experience affection and connection from your partner.
  • If both options feel meaningful, select the one that has a stronger impact on your connection.
  • This quiz aims to help you better understand your love language within a committed partnership.

Enjoy discovering your love language!

Your Privacy Matters

Your quiz results are not shared with anyone unless you choose to do so. The website doesn't save any responses; it is simply to help you understand your love language and relationships.

At the end of the quiz, you will be able to download your results or share them with your loved ones to enhance your relationship.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I choose between the two options for each question? Select the option that resonates most with how you feel loved and valued in your current relationship.
  • What if both options feel relevant to me? It’s common to connect with multiple love languages. Choose the one that feels more meaningful or impactful right now.
  • How long will the quiz take? This quiz typically takes about 5-10 minutes. Take your time and enjoy reflecting on each question.
  • Will my responses be kept confidential? Yes, your answers are not saved or shared with anyone unless you choose to share your results. This quiz is for your personal insight.
